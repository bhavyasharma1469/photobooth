About

photobooth is a free, browser-based photo booth that lets you capture fun photo strips and grids right from your webcam — no app downloads, no sign-ups, no servers involved.

How it works

Choose a layout, pick a filter, and hit the capture button. The countdown timer gives you a moment to pose, then we snap each shot automatically. When you're done, customize your strip with border colors and stickers, then download the final image straight to your device.

100% Private

Everything happens entirely in your browser. Your photos are never uploaded to any server — they exist only in your browser's memory and are gone the moment you close the tab or start a new session.

Technology

Built with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no tracking scripts, no cookies. Just open and snap.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Overview

Your privacy is important to us. This photobooth application is designed with a privacy-first approach — we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or photos.

Data Collection

We collect nothing. Specifically:

  • No photos are uploaded or stored on any server
  • No personal information is collected
  • No cookies are used
  • No tracking cookies or fingerprinting of any kind
  • No personal data is sent to any third party

Camera Access

The application requests access to your device's camera solely to display a live preview and capture photos. The camera feed is processed entirely within your browser and is never transmitted externally. Camera access can be revoked at any time through your browser settings.

Photo Storage

All captured photos exist only in your browser's temporary memory (RAM). They are automatically discarded when you:

  • Close the browser tab
  • Navigate away from the page
  • Start a new photo session

The only way a photo is saved permanently is when you explicitly choose to download it, at which point it is saved directly to your device by your browser — we have no involvement in that process.

Third-Party Services

This application uses two external services:

  • Google Fonts — to load typefaces. Google's privacy policy applies.
  • GoatCounter — a privacy-friendly, open-source analytics tool used solely to count page visits. It uses no cookies, collects no personal data, does not track individual users, and is fully GDPR-compliant. You can read more at goatcounter.com/help/privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Since we collect no data, changes are unlikely.

Contact
